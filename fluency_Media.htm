PONG

PLAY: Normal PONG rules, but in this version, the ball increases speed after each collision. Speed is reset after each score.

Striking the ball closer to the edge of a paddle causes it to rebound at a sharper angle. The closer the ball is to the edge of the paddle, the greater the angle.

The artificial intelligence (AI) is basic. It just tries to match the ball's Y-axis value within a percentage range. This is the only way to defeat the AI (there's a slight chance the ball will miss when it tries to go for an edge strike).

NOTE: There's a known glitch: Sometimes the ball clips through a paddle when it gets going too fast. I believe it has to do with the frame rate and collision detection; I "think" the gap between the ball's current and last positions is greater than the span of the paddle to the edge of the canvas, causing a false score. I probably won't fix it for this assignment. ¯\_(ツ)_/¯
